The Goldman Difference

We believe that every client is one of our most valued commodities. It is our goal to provide you with personalized, comprehensive and innovative legal representation whether you are a victim of a car accident, motorcycle accident, or dealing with the cost and pain associated with a vicious dog bite. We strive to get you the results you deserve. When we take your case our team of skilled attorneys and legal professionals will:


  • Review all accident/ injury details with you
  • Gather all additional facts and evidence related to the accident/injury
  • Listen to any concerns you may have
  • Answer any questions you may have
  • Work on your case to determine cause, fault and/or negligence
  • Carefully gather and review all evidence related to your case
  • Develop a strategy in tandem with you to achieve maximum compensation
  • Work directly with involved insurance companies or take the case to court
